Founders Teams

Founders' organizational structure is broken up into a few main teams. The goal of each team is to bring something to the table that will benefit startups (and our own organization) in a unique way.

The Founders Exec Team is a group of five students that run the internals of Founders. As the faces of the organization, it is their job to oversee the other sub-teams, as well as plan outreach events, recruitment, set up organizational infrastructure, etc. Learn more about the Founders Exec Team here!.

The Community Team focuses on growing the Founders brand. We seek to inform students of the entrepreneurial resources available through our organization. Our team is responsible for all Founders marketing & design material on our social media, newsletter, and website. Learn more about the Founders Community Team, click here!.

The Research and Development Team elevates the Founders value proposition by creating software tools for both internal use and for startups / entrepreneurs to help them scale their companies. They're a team of software developers and designers that keeps Founders as one of the top Computer Science organizations on campus. To learn more about R&D, click here!.

At the end of the day, Founders is an amazing family that shares common interests in entrepreneurship and creating something bigger than ourselves!
