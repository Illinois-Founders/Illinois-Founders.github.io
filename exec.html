The Founders Executive Board is the top of the Founders memeber pyramid. It has one leader per team, and a President / Vice President. Even though Founders has an executive board, all members are valued equally. It's the job of the executive board to make decisions about funding, events planning, projects, outreach, recruitment, and more! We're super excited to see what the Founders Exec Board does this upcoming semester.

If you'd like to join Founders, reach out to us by email or over Facebook. Each semester, we recruit for various teams and event committees - shoot us a message to see how you can help!
